faq

what is block 100,000,000?

the avalanche c-chain's hundred-millionth block. this page counts down to it live, estimating arrival from the current block rate. when it lands, this page becomes the record that it did.

what does the wallet checker do?

paste any avalanche address or .avax name and get its history: first transaction, era, how early it arrived, badges, and realized dex p&l — biggest win, biggest loss, roundtrips, and what it sold too early. read-only. no connect, no signature.

is the page-claim signature safe?

claiming signs one plain-text message to prove you own the address. it is not a transaction: nothing moves, nothing is approved, it costs nothing. this site never asks for token approvals or transfers, ever. the code is open source.

how accurate is the p&l?

fifo realized and unrealized p&l, fees, and external flows come from avalanche-indexed wallet history. unpriced assets are called out instead of becoming zero. story cards use separate transfer and market-price estimates; fallback accounting is labeled on the wallet.
